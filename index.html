Common Violation Scenarios

  • Using copyrighted music in videos without permission

Appeal Process Overview

When you submit an appeal, our team will carefully review your case. The process typically takes 3-5 business days, but complex cases may require additional time.

What happens during the appeal review:

  1. Our team verifies your identity and account information
  2. We review the specific violations cited in your case
  3. We evaluate any evidence or explanations you provide
  4. We check for any previous violations or warnings
  5. A final decision is made based on our policies

Possible outcomes:

  • Appeal Approved: Your account restrictions will be lifted
  • Appeal Denied: The account disabling will proceed as scheduled
  • More Information Needed: We may contact you for additional details

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the appeal process take?

Most appeals are reviewed within 3-5 business days. You will receive a notification once a decision has been made.

Can I submit additional information after filing my appeal?

Yes, you can respond to the confirmation email with any additional information you believe is relevant to your case.

What if my appeal is denied?

If your appeal is denied, the decision is final. You may create a new account but must comply with all Meta policies.

Will my data be preserved during the appeal process?

Yes, your account data will be preserved while your appeal is under review.
